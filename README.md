# AdSpark Lite 🚀

**Create high-performing ad ideas instantly!**

AdSpark Lite is a simple web app that generates ad hooks, UGC-style scripts, headlines, and CTAs for any product or business. Perfect for advertisers, agencies, or anyone who needs quick creative ideas.

---

## 🔹 Features

- Enter product name, target audience, price, and platform
- Generate **20 hooks**, **5 scripts**, **5 headlines**, and **3 CTAs** instantly
- All outputs visible at once for easy copy & paste
- Clean, modern interface optimized for mobile and desktop

---

## 💻 How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser: [GitHub Pages Link]  
2. Fill in the input fields:
   - **Product name**  
   - **Target audience**  
   - **Price**  
   - **Platform** (TikTok, Meta, YouTube, etc.)  
3. Tap **Generate**  
4. Copy the results and use them in your ad campaigns  

---

## 📱 Tips for iPhone

- Use Safari for best performance  
- Rotate to landscape for easier viewing  
- Tap **Share → Add to Home Screen** to save it as a mini app
